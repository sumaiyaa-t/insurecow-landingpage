About InsureCow

InsureCow is a cattle wellbeing monitoring and insurance platform that is playing a significant role in protecting and ensuring the asset value of large cattle for livestock farmers. InsureCow is poised to become a game-changer in ensuring financial access for the livestock sector of the country.
